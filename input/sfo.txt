San Francisco, is a hilly city on the tip of a peninsula, surrounded by the Pacific Ocean and the Bay. It's known for its fog, iconic Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars and colorful Victorian houses. The Financial District's Transamerica Pyramid is its most distinctive skyscraper. In the bay sits Alcatraz Island, site of the notorious former prison. Lombard Street is famous for a steep, one-block section with eight hairpin turns. Tourists also flood to Pier 39, which offers dining, shopping, and beautiful views of the bay and seals.
